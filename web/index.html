What is IkaPayFi

A private, bridgeless PayFi super app on Solana.

IkaPayFi is a private bridgeless PayFi super app built on Solana using Ika dWallets and Encrypt FHE. It lets freelancers, DAOs, remote teams and families receive and manage money from any blockchain — Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins — inside a single programmable private vault, without bridges or centralized custodians.

Unlike traditional crypto wallets where balances and transactions are publicly visible, IkaPayFi keeps balances, payment amounts, allocation rules and spending limits fully encrypted on-chain via Encrypt FHE — while the policy logic still executes verifiably on Solana.

Inflows from any chain settle into your Ika dWallet, an encrypted programmable account bound to your Solana identity. From there, an on-chain policy engine automatically routes funds: payroll splits, savings buckets, family remittances, subscription payments, spending limits and shared treasury access — all enforced directly on Solana with web2-style passkey login.

In simple terms: IkaPayFi turns Solana into a private global banking layer for payroll, remittances and programmable money movement across chains.
